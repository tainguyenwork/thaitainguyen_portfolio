About Marianna

Digital Fashion Designer and Technical Apparel Developer with expertise in CLO3D virtual garment simulation, tech packs, and pattern making. Experienced in lingerie, tailoring, and ready-to-wear with international background in Miami and Madrid.

My passion lies in bridging the gap between creative vision and technical execution through digital innovation. By leveraging cutting-edge technology like CLO3D, I help brands reduce physical sampling, accelerate product development, and create more sustainable fashion workflows.

With experience across diverse markets and garment categories, I bring a global perspective to every project, ensuring designs are both commercially viable and creatively compelling.
