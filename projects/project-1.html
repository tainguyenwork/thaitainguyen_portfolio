Client / Context

Snowpeak Design Challenge

Services Provided

Digital Prototyping, Pattern Construction, Renderings

My Role

Digital Apparel Designer

Timeline

Winter 2025

The Challenge & Approach

The objective of this design challenge was to develop a high-performance winterwear jacket that matches technical cold-weather protection with a sleek urban aesthetic. The jacket needed to support anatomical movement while incorporating layered waterproof partitions.

Research & Structural Design

I began by researching traditional Japanese sub-zero gear architectures, mapping heat retention patterns across active muscle groups. Standard zippers and seams were optimized using custom 2D templates in Gerber CAD, then exported to CLO3D.

Digital Simulation

Through advanced 3D virtual fittings, fabric tension and material stretch properties were analyzed. I applied custom wind simulations to evaluate drape behavior under pressure, adjusting panel segmentation to eliminate physical friction points.

Outcome & Impact

The final digital twin achieved 100% assembly accuracy, reducing sampling material waste to zero. Detailed manufacturing specifications were packaged into an export-ready Tech Pack.

Key Deliverables

  • High-fidelity CLO3D Sim Files
  • Graded 2D Pattern Files (DXF/AAMA)
  • Fully Detailed Tech Packs
  • Render Materials & Handoff specs
  • Fabric drape stress analyses
