Client / Context

Draping & Shape Studies

Services Provided

Pattern Draping, Color Grading, 3D Fit Validation

My Role

Pattern Making Specialist

Timeline

Spring 2025

The Challenge & Approach

The goal of this project was to explore structural color-blocking inspired by 1980s retro sportswear, focusing on panel joints and drape weights. This study maps how contrasting seams react when draped on active, moving avatars.

Creative Research & Color Mapping

I studied vintage windbreakers and track pants, digitizing traditional pattern layouts. Color schemes were carefully selected to represent retro aesthetics while fitting cleanly into a contemporary, premium context.

3D Draping & Simulation

Using CLO3D's physical properties engine, different weights of nylon and cotton were simulated. Seam puckering and panel stretch tolerances were finely tuned to guarantee that the panels lay flat across physical production seams.

Outcome & Impact

The study successfully demonstrated how color block lines can be strategically placed along natural biomechanical lines, improving comfort while maintaining structural symmetry.

Key Deliverables

  • Colorblock pattern templates
  • CLO3D dynamic simulations
  • Detailed stitching & grading specs
  • CMYK/RGB color palette mappings
  • Fabric texture library packages
