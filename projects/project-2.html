Futuristic Streetwear Tracksuit

Client / Context

Digital Fashion Collection

Services Provided

3D Draping, Texturing, Lighting Setup, Walk-cycle animation

My Role

Lead 3D Fashion Designer

Timeline

Spring 2025

The Challenge & Approach

This project focused on designing a modular, futuristic streetwear tracksuit with custom utility attachments. The silhouette needed to appear heavy and structured while retaining natural, soft movement under active poses.

Creative Concept & Styling

I chose a dark carbon-fiber texture scheme layered with custom straps and elastic buckles. The design references cyber-industrial silhouettes combined with classic sport geometries to establish a progressive design statement.

Pattern Design & Construction

Patterns were drafted to support complex raglan sleeves and ergonomic knee articulations. These details ensure that real-world sewing lines align perfectly, providing accurate simulation references in CLO3D.

Outcome & Impact

The virtual prototype was used directly in lookbook renders and motion graphic promotions, resulting in a zero-physical sampling loop and 80% faster design sign-off.

Key Deliverables

  • High-res CLO3D simulations
  • Anatomical avatars and poses
  • Custom trim & hardware designs
  • Production-ready tech packs
  • Marketing lookbook renderings
